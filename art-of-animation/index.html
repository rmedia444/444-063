The Art of Animation

Animation is the process of creating motion and shape change illusions by displaying a series of static images. It combines creativity and technical skill to bring characters, stories, and worlds to life in a dynamic and engaging way.
The art of animation encompasses a wide range of styles and techniques, from traditional hand-drawn animation to computer-generated imagery (CGI). Animators use principles of movement, timing, and storytelling to create compelling narratives that resonate with audiences of all ages. Animation is used in films, television shows, video games, and advertisements, showcasing its versatility and impact on popular culture.
